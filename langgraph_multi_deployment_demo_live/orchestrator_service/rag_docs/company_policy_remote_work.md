# Remote Work Policy (Internal)

## Eligibility
- Full-time employees may request remote work after 30 days of employment.
- Contractors are not eligible unless explicitly approved by HR and the team’s director.

## Working Hours
- Core collaboration hours: 10:00–16:00 local time (employee’s location).
- Outside core hours, async communication is preferred.

## Equipment & Reimbursement
- Company provides a standard laptop and security accessories (e.g., MFA token).
- One-time home-office setup reimbursement: up to $300 (chair, monitor, desk).
- Internet reimbursement: up to $30/month with receipt.

## Travel & Onsite Expectations
- Remote employees may be required onsite for quarterly planning.
- Travel must be approved by the manager before booking.

## Security Requirements
- Work must be performed on managed devices only.
- Do not store secrets in local notes or plain-text files.
- Use company VPN when accessing internal systems.

## Contacts / Escalation
- HR: hr@company.example
- Security: security@company.example
